# Project Overview
GreenLife is an NGO that works on environmental sustuinability, we run recycling awareness programs, plants trees and organize community clean up events. This website was built to help spread the word about our work , to get more people to volunteer and encourage donatations for our projects. it has a clean modern design that's easy to use.

---
# Website goals and objectives

## Goals
- To spread awaresness about protecting the environment.
- To get more people involved as volunteers.
- To attract donations and other forms of support
- To keep everyone updated about what we're working on.

## Objectives
- It makes it simple for people to get in touch with us.
- Visitors can sign up to volunteer directly on the site.
- People can donate online without hassle.
- It presents projects and events in a clear and organised way.

---

# Pages included
- Home- it gives a quick into to the organisation and showcases key projects.
- About- it tells the story behind the organisation and outlines its mission and vision.
- Projects- lists current and past environmental projects and events.
- Get involved- let visitors sign up to volunteer or to make a donation.
- Contact- Shares contact info and includes a form reaching out.

---

# Website features
- Navigation menu that is available on every page for easy browsing.
- Volunteer form where people can sign up to volunteer.
- Online donation form that allows users to donate directly on the site.
- Contact form which is a simple way for visitors to get in touch with the organisation.
- Photo gallery section to display images from events and projects.
- A space to post and view news about upcoming and past events.
- The site adjusts to look good on phones, tablets and computers.
- Buttons and forms that let users engage with the site.
- A look and feel that reflects the environmental focus or the organisation.

# User experience

The GreenLife Foundation website was built with the user in mind to:
1. Make browsing simple and intuitive.
2. Give it a clean modern and professional look.
3. Work smoothly on both mobile devices and computers
4. Let visitors find information quickly and without hassle.
5. Encourage people to get involved with the comunity.
6. Boost engagement by using easy to use forms and interactive buttons.
